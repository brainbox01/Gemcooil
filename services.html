Thermal Electricity

Beyond generating electricity by so-called “conventional” power plants, GEMCO operates combined cycle and cogeneration plants which use natural gas, resulting in diversified electricity production with a low level of CO2 emissions. These plants provide improved energy efficiency, help secure the power supply and allow a very rapid response to peaks in electricity consumption.
